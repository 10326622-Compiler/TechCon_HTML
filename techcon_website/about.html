Our History

TechCon was founded in 2018 with a vision to create a collaborative space for African tech professionals. Starting as a small gathering of 50 people, it has grown into a major international event, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

Our goal is to foster innovation, promote diversity in tech, and provide actionable insights into emerging technologies. We strive to be the catalyst for the next wave of technological breakthroughs.

Notable Past Speakers

We've hosted some of the brightest minds in the industry, including:

  • Alex Johnson: A leading software architect who spoke on serverless computing in 2022.

  • Maria Garcia: A celebrated cybersecurity expert who discussed zero-trust architecture in 2023.
